## 🚀 How to Run the Client

1. **Launch Your Launcher**: Open your launcher.
2. **Run version 1.8.9 with forge**: Click start! Since the newly compiled JAR has already been copied directly to your mods folder, the client will automatically load the brand new client engine.
3. **Open ClickGUI**: Join any world/server, and press **Right Shift** or you can chat " .bind ClickGui "your key" " to open UI.
4. **Drag & Search**: Enjoy the fast search, toggle modules, expand custom settings, and drag the UI to your favorite screen positions!
